  <div class="ppt-line"></div>
  <div class="ppt-body" style="margin-top:16px;">
    <div style="font-size:0.7em; font-weight:800; color:#1f4ba5; margin-bottom:8px;">
      What is the goal?
    </div>
    <div style="font-size:0.6em; line-height:1.42; background:#f7f8fc; border:1px solid #d9deea; border-radius:12px; padding:12px 14px; margin-bottom:12px;">
      The goal of the course project is to solve an NLP task or problem using NLP techniques and methods.
      It is a good opportunity to apply what you have learned in class to an in-depth application problem
      and/or a small research problem.
    </div>
    <div style="font-size:0.7em; font-weight:800; color:#1f4ba5; margin-bottom:8px;">
      How should you choose a topic?
    </div>
    <div style="font-size:0.6em; line-height:1.42; background:#fbfbfd; border:1px solid #d9deea; border-radius:12px; padding:12px 14px; margin-bottom:12px;">
      You and your teammates should choose something that you are genuinely interested in or passionate about.
      A good project topic is usually easier to sustain over several weeks.
    </div>
    <div style="font-size:0.7em; font-weight:800; color:#1f4ba5; margin-bottom:8px;">
      What should you keep in mind?
    </div>
    <div style="font-size:0.6em; line-height:1.42; background:#fff8f0; border:1px solid #eed9b6; border-radius:12px; padding:12px 14px;">
      <div style="margin-bottom:6px;">• use a <b>well-defined dataset</b></div>
      <div style="margin-bottom:6px;">• keep the project scope <b>manageable</b></div>
      <div>• consider model size, training time, and available resources</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

        <div style="margin-bottom:6px;"><b>2. Reproducing a recent paper</b></div>
        <div>Reproduce a recent ACL / NAACL / EMNLP / COLM paper from the past 5 years. NeurIPS / ICLR / ICML papers are also acceptable if they are clearly NLP-related.</div>
      </div>

        <div style="margin-bottom:4px;">• design or apply a model to a new task/problem</div>
        <div style="margin-bottom:4px;">• improve an existing model</div>
        <div style="margin-bottom:4px;">• conduct experimental or method analysis of an NLP model</div>
        <div>• or propose another reasonable project idea</div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div style="flex:0.95;">
      <div style="font-size:0.7em; font-weight:800; color:#1f4ba5; margin-bottom:8px;">

        <div style="margin-bottom:6px;">• each project should be completed in a <b>small group</b></div>
        <div style="margin-bottom:6px;">• <b>no more than 5</b> group members (3 members are strongly recommended)</div>
